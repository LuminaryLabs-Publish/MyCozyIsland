## Finding

`coreTransactionLedger.applyOnce()` records an operation only after its callback returns. Farming and foraging callbacks execute nested inventory operations before the parent plot/node mutation and parent ledger record. No candidate state, participant rollback, parent pending record, atomic commit result or committed-frame revision protects the resulting multi-owner transaction.
