```txt

  -> cozyInventory.applyChanges
  -> agriculture.commitPlan
```

## Required migration additions

- Classify every legacy `cozy-farming` operation.
- Map compatible operations to an explicit migration receipt or preserve them in a read-only legacy ledger.
- Detect parent/child history that cannot be proven complete.
- Quarantine conflicting records instead of silently admitting them.
- Record source schema, source checksum, migration policy version and target Agriculture fingerprint.
- Validate authentic save-v1 fixtures generated by the removed farming implementation.
- Verify post-migration retries do not duplicate seed debits or harvest rewards.

## Required rollback additions

- Capture event-queue and journal boundaries before settlement.
- Buffer Agriculture events until the aggregate commit succeeds.
- Restore exact participant revisions or allocate a declared rollback generation.
- Publish one typed rollback result.
- Prevent save, HUD and render admission from candidate or indeterminate state.
