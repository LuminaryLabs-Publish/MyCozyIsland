## Finding

Both sides send to `location.origin` and filter by `event.source`, but neither inbound handler verifies `event.origin`. More importantly, protocol messages are not bound to a protocol version, preload attempt, iframe/document generation, entry attempt, message identity, sequence or expected revision. The parent can reveal after a 900 ms timeout without a terminal child result, and the child acknowledges entry before a post-resume visible frame exists.
